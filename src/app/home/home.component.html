Securely share your files without server

ShareTC is a service allowing you to securely share file with someone else. It works without any server, your browser will act as the sender, so keep your browser open while sending. The service do not use any server thanks to the RTC peer-to-peer technology. You'll need to communicate the generated key for your receiver to be able to download file.
